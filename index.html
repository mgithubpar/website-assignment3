Queens Park Cafe

Modern Australian cuisine

Open since 2009, Queen’s Park Café is a family owned, 200 seater café nestled in Queen’s Park overlooking the city of Ipswich. The café is located within walking distance to not only the CBD but all Queen’s Park facilities such as the playground and Nature Centre (animal park). What can our cafe provide

One of our most popular packages is our vintage high tea which includes a beautiful table setting and delectable finger food.

Our catering menu has options for corporate meetings, bus tours and a Christmas menu that will have you coming back each year. Our catering menu has been designed to suit functions with diverse age groups and most allergies. Whether it's a casual gathering, a special event or a formal work meeting, we can cater for most requirements.

Not only can you hire our private function room which comfortably seats 50 and displays beautiful native flora and fauna on the walls, we can also cater for functions held in the environmental education centre next door to the cafe (please phone Ipswich City Council on 3810 6666 to book). The catering menu has a variety of options for off-site catering as well.

For more information on our catering menu, please see the terms and conditions or call us on 3281 5167.
